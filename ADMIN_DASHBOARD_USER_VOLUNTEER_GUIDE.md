## FAQs for Dashboard Users

**Q: How long does item collection take?**
A: Typically 3-7 days from submission to pickup date.

**Q: Can I change my item after submission?**
A: Contact admin while status is "Pending". Once assigned, changes require approval.

**Q: How do volunteers get assigned?**
A: Admin reviews area, availability, and item type, then assigns best match.

**Q: What if my item isn't collected?**
A: Admin will contact you. Check dashboard for updates or reach support.

**Q: How are volunteers vetted?**
A: Applications are reviewed for reliability, background check, and commitment.

**Q: Can I volunteer again after rejection?**
A: Yes, you can reapply after addressing feedback.

**Q: Where do collected items go?**
A: Partner NGOs handle proper recycling/refurbishment. Track in dashboard.

**Q: How do I get rewards?**
A: Rewards auto-awarded when items are collected. Check profile for status.
